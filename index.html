Privacy Policy

Puzzleshift  ·  Effective Date: March 28, 2026

✔ No account or personal information required

1. Introduction

Welcome to Puzzleshift. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

Puzzleshift does not require any account, login, or personal information. The only data generated is an anonymous device identifier — a randomly generated ID (UUID) created the first time you use VS Mode. This ID:

  • Is generated entirely at random on your device
  • Is not linked to your name, email address, or any personal information
  • Is used solely to identify your device within a VS Mode game session
  • Is stored locally on your device and transmitted to our game servers only when you use VS Mode

When you create or join a VS Mode room, the following game session data is stored on our servers:

  • Your anonymous device ID
  • Room code and game configuration (puzzle image, board size)
  • Game state and completion times
  • Session timestamps (when the room was created and last active)

We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any contact information
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Advertising IDs or device fingerprints
  • Photos, videos, or files from your device
  • Browsing history or behavioral analytics

3. How We Use This Information

The anonymous device ID and room data are used exclusively to operate the VS Mode multiplayer feature — specifically to match two players in the same room and determine the outcome of the game. This data is not used for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond enabling real-time gameplay.

Game room data is temporary and may be automatically deleted from our servers after a period of inactivity.

4. Third-Party Services

Puzzleshift uses Supabase (supabase.com) as its backend database and real-time infrastructure to power VS Mode. When you use VS Mode, game session data is transmitted to and stored on Supabase servers. Supabase's privacy practices are governed by their own Privacy Policy.

The App does not integrate any advertising networks, analytics SDKs, social login providers, or any other third-party data collection services.

5. App Permissions

The App requests the following device permissions to provide its core functionality:

  • Internet access — required for VS Mode to connect to game servers
  • Network state — used to check connectivity before attempting multiplayer
  • Audio settings — used to manage in-game sound playback

No data derived from these permissions is stored or shared beyond what is described in this policy.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages and does not collect any personal information from any user, including children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and have concerns about your child's use of this App, please contact us.

7. Data Security

Single-player game data is stored entirely on your device and never transmitted externally. VS Mode session data transmitted to Supabase is protected using industry-standard encryption (TLS) in transit. No passwords, financial data, or personal identifiers are ever stored on our servers.

8. Data Retention

Your anonymous device ID is stored locally on your device until you uninstall the App. VS Mode room data on our servers is temporary and may be purged automatically after a period of inactivity. You may contact us at any time to request deletion of any data associated with your anonymous device ID.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this document. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

10. Your Rights

Since Puzzleshift does not collect personal information, there are no personal profiles to access or correct. If you wish to request deletion of the anonymous game data associated with your device, please contact us with your device's anonymous player ID and we will remove it from our servers.
